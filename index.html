WOW Westminster: An Archive Exhibit

WOW Westminster is an installation art piece by Brazillian artisit Jos√© Resende located in New Westminster, B.C. presented by The Vancouver Biennale. For more information, click here. What you see here is an exhibit that uses an archive of photos of the installation. The installation is a large "W" made out of shipping containers. This archive of the artwork asks the viewer to think about point-of-view and the specifics of how we look at texts. At the same time, it invites viewers to assemble their own narrative about the images of the exhibit.

This is part of a series of exhibits. Check out the other one here: Wow Westminster and Vancouver Specials.
